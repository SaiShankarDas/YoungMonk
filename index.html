Our Destinations

Experience the magic of the Himalayas at our two stunning locations, each offering unique adventures and breathtaking views.

Jibhi

Jibhi Valley Paradise

Nestled in the heart of Himachal Pradesh, our Jibhi location offers pristine nature, traditional architecture, and endless trekking opportunities. Perfect for digital nomads and nature lovers.

Trekking Nature Photography

Dharamkot

Spiritual Mountain Retreat

Located above McLeod Ganj, Dharamkot is known for its spiritual vibe, yoga retreats, and stunning views of the Dhauladhar range. A perfect place for meditation and self-discovery.

Yoga Meditation Spiritual
